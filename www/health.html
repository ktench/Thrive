  • How much water are you drinking?

    You're probably not getting enough water every day. Did you know that at the minimum, you're supposed to drink half your body weight in ounces of water each day? Many dieticians actually recommoend that you drink at least 9 cups a day. That means if you're 130 pounds, you should be drinking at least 65 ounces of water. Thats approximately 8 and a half cups of water a day, at the minimum. I know, math is hard. That's why this handy section will tell you how much water you should drink.
